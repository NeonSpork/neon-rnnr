# neon rnnr ⚡

> A minimalist, offline-first retrowave weekly checklist PWA built for training and running routines.

**neon rnnr** is a lightweight, zero-dependency Progressive Web App designed to keep your weekly athletic habits on track. Built with a sleek retrowave aesthetic, it stores all your data locally on your device and automatically resets your checkmarks every Monday at 00:00 local time so you start every week fresh.

DISCLAIMER:
Heavily vibe coded, use at your own discression and at your own risk.
---

## ✨ Features

- **Automated Monday Reset:** Checkmarks reset automatically at 00:00 local time every Monday morning. Your task list stays preserved permanently—only the status clears.
- **100% Offline & Private:** Operates entirely in your browser using `localStorage`. No accounts, no backend servers, and no data tracking.
- **Retrowave Aesthetic:** Deep violet dark mode with glowing cyan and hot pink accents.
- **Customizable:** Toggle **Edit Mode** via the pencil icon (`✏️`) to add new tasks, edit existing text, reorder items, or delete tasks.
- **Native PWA Support:** Installable directly to your iOS or Android home screen with a dedicated app icon and full-screen experience.

## 📲 Installation Instructions

### **Android (Google Pixel / Chrome)**
1. Open the live app URL in **Google Chrome**.
2. Tap the **three-dot menu (⋮)** in the top right corner.
3. Tap **Install app** (or **Add to Home screen** $\rightarrow$ **Install**).
4. The **neon rnnr** app icon with the glowing teal triangle will appear in your app drawer and home screen.

---

### **iOS (iPhone / Safari)**
1. Open the live app URL in **Safari** *(must use Safari for iOS installation)*.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow pointing up at the bottom bar).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add** in the top right corner.
